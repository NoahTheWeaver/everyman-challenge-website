What Makes It Different

BECAUSE YOU DON'T NEED ANOTHER "MEN'S PROGRAM"...

...the Everyman Challenge is built different — not one more thing for you to manage, but a guided path that turns the men already sitting in your pews into disciples who make disciples.

Built for churches

It's built for the local church — not a parachurch program your men do on the side. The Every Man Challenge plugs into your ministry and strengthens it from the inside out.

Your men lead your men

No celebrity speaker. No new staff hire. You identify the leaders already in your pews; we train them as Guides to lead groups of 12–15 men, shoulder to shoulder.

Designed to multiply

Every man who finishes is equipped to lead the next group. One group becomes two, two become four — disciples who make disciples, without adding to your plate.

A path, not an event

Six months of sustained discipleship — brotherhood, scripture, prayer, and family leadership — replaces the weekend high that fades by Monday.
